These photographs are high resolution color photos of typed and handwritten documents that need to be transformed into scan quality documents.  The steps involved are:
  • Convert color to grayscale (128 shades or less)
  • Remove background noise and speckles/flecks/etc
  • Reduce size of images for easier distribution and lower bandwidth usage (currently ~40 GB for set of images)
